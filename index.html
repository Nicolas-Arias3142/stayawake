    About BMX

    Bmx is an activity and sport that does not require any team or coach, you are in control of yourself and the speed of your progression. Bmx is a relaxing yet exhilarating sport and you can take it as seriously as you want. There are many forms of BMX and different ways to do all of them. The most popular BMX riders are the most creative and add new styles, tricks and mindsets to the sport, further edvancing the sport!
